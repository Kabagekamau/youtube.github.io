About Kabage Kamau

Welcome to the official YouTube channel of Kabage Kamau, a devoted Gospel music minister, singer, songwriter, and recording artist. Through powerful worship and praise songs, Kabage spreads the transformative message of Jesus Christ to millions around the world.


With over 48 million subscribers and hundreds of millions of views, Kabage's ministry reaches hearts across the globe with biblical truths, inspiring testimonies, and soul-stirring melodies that bring people closer to God.


From his debut album "Savior" to his latest releases in "New Beginnings," every song is crafted to glorify God and minister to souls seeking hope, healing, and salvation through Jesus Christ.
